Performance & Range

Max Speed: ~25 mph on level surfaces (varies by setup).

Suspension tuned for both street and light off-road comfort.

Turn Signals & Brake Lights

Horn

Side & Rearview Mirrors

DOT-Approved Tires

Road-ready tires with DOT street rating
(Availability depends on dealer / state laws.)

Summary — Why D-MAX XT4

The Evolution D-MAX XT4 blends off-road power with street-legal capability, offering a dual-motor 4WD system, premium infotainment, comfort tech, and utility features all for around $15,595 before options and fees.
