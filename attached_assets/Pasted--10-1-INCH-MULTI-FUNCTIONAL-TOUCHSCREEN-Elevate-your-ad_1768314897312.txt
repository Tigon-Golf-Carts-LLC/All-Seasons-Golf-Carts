Electric Power Steering
D-Max’s Electric Power Steering (EPS) gives you comfort, control and feel-of-the-road, which is a technology that will define the future. lt eliminates the need for a power steering pump, hoses, hydraulic fluids, and a drive belt and pulley on the engine. As a result, electric power steering is energy efficient and environmentally compatible-while offering extras like simplified tuning, packaging flexibility and engine-off steering assist.
