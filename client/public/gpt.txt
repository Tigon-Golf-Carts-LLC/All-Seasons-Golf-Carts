# TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Drive System
The EVolution D-MAX uses a dual AC motor 4WD system. Each axle has its own 5kW motor, providing a combined 10kW of power. This delivers superior traction compared to single-motor golf carts.

## Battery Technology
48V lithium-ion battery pack provides:
- Extended range (40-60 miles)
- Fast charging capability
- Longer lifespan than lead-acid
- Lighter weight for better performance
- Consistent power delivery

## Suspension System
Independent front and rear suspension provides:
- Smoother ride quality
- Better handling on rough terrain
- Improved stability
- Reduced wear on components

## Braking System
4-wheel hydraulic disc brakes offer:
- Superior stopping power
- Consistent performance in all conditions
- Low maintenance requirements
- Regenerative braking for extended range

# INDUSTRY TERMINOLOGY
# ============================================================

4WD - Four Wheel Drive: Power delivered to all four wheels
4X4 - Same as 4WD, indicates all-wheel-drive capability
LSV - Low Speed Vehicle: Street legal vehicle with max speed of 25 MPH
NEV - Neighborhood Electric Vehicle: Another term for street legal golf cart
AC Motor - Alternating Current motor, more efficient than DC
Lithium-ion - Modern battery technology with high energy density
Regenerative Braking - System that recharges battery during braking
Independent Suspension - Each wheel moves independently for better ride
Ground Clearance - Distance between ground and lowest point of vehicle
